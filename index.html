Maxwell Citron



I am an artist-engineer working across music, material, and computer science.

I co-founded and serve as CEO of everywhere. Our work has been featured in Wired, Inc, Forbes, and adopted by Chappell Roan, Amazon, Google, Rivian, Coach, Chanel, etc.

My life in music includes commissioned work for Rolls Royce at the The Royal Society of the Arts, Brain-Computer-Music research at the MIT Media Lab, and project features on NPR, PBS, and the BBC.

I have a longterm research interest in universal semantic data, graph datastructures, and object-oriented ontology.

I studied Music Science at Stanford's Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics (CCRMA).
